# R-026-005: PSP-Lite Registration Requirement

From 2027-07-01 onward, every stored-value kiosk operator must hold PSP-Lite
registration before operating. A stored-value operator without PSP-Lite
registration is prohibited unless a transition rule expressly allows continued
operation.

This rule repeals and overrides the campus wallet exemption in `R-026-001` for
transactions on or after 2027-07-01.

Condition marker: `C-026-psp-lite-registration`.

Effective date: 2027-07-01.
