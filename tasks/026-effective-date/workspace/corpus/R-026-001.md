# R-026-001: Campus Wallet Exemption

Before 2027-07-01, an education-campus stored-value wallet with monthly
transaction volume below 50,000 credits may operate without PSP-Lite
registration.

This exemption is repealed for transactions on or after 2027-07-01 by
`R-026-005`.

Condition marker: `C-026-campus-wallet-low-volume`.

Effective date: 2026-01-01.
