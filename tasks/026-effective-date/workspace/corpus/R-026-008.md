# R-026-008: Existing Operator Transition Period

An operator that was already active before 2027-07-01 and filed a PSP-Lite
application by 2027-07-15 may continue operating without completed
registration only through 2027-09-30.

After 2027-09-30, the transition permission expires. Continued operation then
requires completed PSP-Lite registration under `R-026-005`.

Condition marker: `C-026-transition-filing`.

Effective date: 2027-07-01.
