# Scenario: Campus wallet after the transition window

HarborPay operates stored-value kiosks on a university campus. On
2027-10-01, it wants to continue offering small student wallet transfers.

Facts supplied by compliance:

- HarborPay was operating before 2027-07-01.
- HarborPay filed a PSP-Lite application on 2027-07-10.
- HarborPay has not yet received PSP-Lite registration.
- Monthly transaction volume is below 50,000 credits.
- The service is campus-only and does not support payroll, insurance, or
  remittances.

Decide whether HarborPay may continue operating on 2027-10-01 under the rule
corpus.
