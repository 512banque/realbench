# Scenario: Residential grocery sidewalk cart

On 2028-06-03, BentoBox wants to use autonomous sidewalk delivery carts to
carry sealed consumer groceries inside a residential green zone.

Facts supplied by operations:

- The vehicle is a low-speed sidewalk cart, not an aerial drone.
- The goods are sealed consumer groceries.
- The route stays inside a residential green zone.
- The operation is not on a university campus, not in a commercial blue zone,
  and not inside a warehouse.
- The cart does not carry calibration modules, waste, security equipment,
  passengers, or controlled chemicals.

Decide whether the operation is covered by the rule corpus.
