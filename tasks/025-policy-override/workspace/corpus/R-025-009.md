# R-025-009: Emergency Utility Night Override

An emergency-certified drone carrier may perform a night flight carrying a
calibration module when the module is tied to a utility incident identifier.
The cold-chain endorsement and technical handling manifest requirements in
`R-025-004` still apply.

When all requirements in this rule and `R-025-004` are satisfied, this rule
overrides the night-operation prohibition in `R-025-006`.

Condition markers: `C-025-emergency-certification`,
`C-025-utility-incident`.

Effective date: 2027-09-01.
